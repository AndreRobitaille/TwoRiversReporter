10.CITY COUNCIL - FORMAL ITEMS

  1. A.26-006 Resolution Adopting Proposed Settlement Agreement Between Two Rivers Fire Fighters IAFF Local 423, AFL-CIO and the City of Two Rivers

    Summary: The tentative agreement includes scheduled wage adjustments and targeted updates to benefits, training compensation, and lateral hiring provisions to help the City recruit and retain qualified firefighter-paramedics. The agreement was negotiated to remain competitive in the regional labor market while maintaining responsible, predictable long-term costs for taxpayers.

    Recommended Action:
    Motion to waive reading and adopt the resolution

  2. B.26-007 New Class "B" / "Class B" License for Spices of the East LLC, dba The Spices Restaurant & Bar Two Rivers, 1210 Washington Street, Basudev Adhikari, Agent for a period ending June 30, 2026

    Summary: This license will allow Spices Restaurant to serve fermented malt beverages, liquor, and wine in their establishment.

    Recommended Action:
    Motion to authorize the City Clerk to issue the license pending approval of inspections and background check

11.CITY COUNCIL - DISCUSSION ITEMS

  1. A.Review of 2025 Goals and 2026 Goals

    Summary: The City Council will hold a discussion on proposed goals and priorities for 2026 and review goals from 2025, focusing on organizational stability, service delivery, and long-term community outcomes.

  2. B.Tax Increment Finance Strategy for 2026

    Summary: The City of Two Rivers operates 12 Tax Increment Districts with a recent staff review of their performance to ensure the continue to meet development and fiscal goals.  Based on 2025 data, City staff recommend the early termination of underperforming districts and amendments to active TID's to reflect recent State law changes.
